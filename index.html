Redesign JGU Project

Welcome to the Redesign JGU Project, an open source design initiative for ecological redevelopment of our university! We invite students, faculty, staff, and the wider community to contribute their ideas and expertise to create a more sustainable and environmentally friendly campus.

Contribute to the Project

We encourage you to review these documents, share your feedback, and contribute your own ideas for the redesign of JGU campus to make it more sustainable and environmentally conscious.

About the Project

The Redesign JGU Project aims to create a comprehensive plan for the ecological redevelopment of our university campus. Our goal is to incorporate sustainable practices and design principles that promote environmental stewardship, conservation, and resilience. By harnessing the collective creativity and expertise of our community, we aim to create a model for sustainable campus design that can be replicated in other educational institutions.
